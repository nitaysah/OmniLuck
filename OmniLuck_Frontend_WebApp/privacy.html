Welcome to OmniLuck ("we," "our," or "us"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with accurate astrological insights and secure account management:

  • Personal Identity Information: Name, Email Address (used for account creation and authentication).

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To perform astronomical calculations required for your personalized luck score.
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To maintain your preferences and history across devices.

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Third Parties

We use trusted third-party services to ensure your data is secure:

  • Google Firebase: We use Firebase for secure authentication and database storage. Your data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate data.
  • Request Deletion: You may request the full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us or using the deletion feature in the app settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our project repository or support channels.
